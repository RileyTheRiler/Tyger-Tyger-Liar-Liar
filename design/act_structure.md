# Act Structure: The Kaltvik Investigation

## Overview
The narrative is structured into three acts, corresponding to the deepening of the "Polar Night" and the deterioration of the player's grasp on reality.

**Total Duration**: 67 Days (Narrative Time)

---

## Act 1: The Long Twilight (Days 1-14)
**Theme**: Isolation & Friction
**Goal**: Establish the setting, the mystery, and the "Unreliable Lens".

### Narrative Goals
1.  **Arrival**: Trap the player in Kaltvik (Ferry leaves/breaks).
2.  **The Hook**: The disappearance of Old Tom.
3.  **Introduction**: Meet the core cast (Maude, Dr. Correl, Sheriff).
4.  **System Tutorial**: Teach the player to use Lenses/Skills to investigate.

### Key Scenes
*   `intro_arrival`: The Ferry landing. First taste of the atmosphere.
*   `lobby_interrogation`: First social combat with Maude.
*   `clinic_autopsy`: Examining evidence that doesn't make sense.
*   **Inciting Incident**: The Storm hits. The sun sets for the last time (Day 7).

### Agency Points
*   Choosing an initial "Ally" (Maude vs. Sheriff vs. Doctor).
*   First major Theory formation on the Board.

---

## Act 2: The Deep Dark (Days 15-45)
**Theme**: Paranoia & Divergence
**Goal**: Break the player's reliance on one lens. Force them to question their perception.

### Narrative Goals
1.  **Escalation**: The phenomena get worse. "The Green Pulse" starts.
2.  **Investigation**: Uncovering the history of the "1998 Event".
3.  **Midpoint Climax**: The Radio Tower Ascent.
4.  **The Split**: The town factions fracture.

### Key Scenes
*   `radio_tower_climb`: A physical and psychological ascent. The player makes contact (or thinks they do).
*   `woods_clearing`: Encountering something that defies the Skeptic lens.
*   `town_hall_meeting`: Social combat against the town council.
*   `dream_sequence_01`: First direct intrusion of the "Haunted" past.

### Perspective Divergence
*   **Believer**: Starts seeing the Entity as a god/force.
*   **Skeptic**: Uncovers a military experiment (Project NORTHSTAR).
*   **Haunted**: Realizes this is personal punishment for past sins.

---

## Act 3: The Breaking Point (Days 46-66)
**Theme**: Synthesis or Collapse
**Goal**: The convergence of timelines. The player must choose a truth.

### Narrative Goals
1.  **Crisis**: The town is dying. Resources (heat/light) are failing.
2.  **The Revelation**: The true nature of the "Entity" is revealed (relative to the chosen lens).
3.  **The Choice**: Save the town, save yourself, or burn it all.

### Key Scenes
*   `lighthouse_basement`: The source of the signal.
*   `frozen_lake`: The final confrontation with the antagonist.
*   `hospital_ruins`: Confronting the "Haunted" origin point.

---

## Epilogue: Day 67 (Dawn?)
**Theme**: Aftermath
**Goal**: Show the consequences of the player's defining choice.

*   **Ending A (Rational)**: Rescue arrives. Kaltvik was a tragedy of isolation and hysteria.
*   **Ending B (Supernatural)**: Kaltvik is gone. You are the herald.
*   **Ending C (Personal)**: You find peace (or oblivion) in the freeze.
